Disha Chawhan is a romance writer who loves stories shaped by fate, chance encounters, and emotional connections. Her work explores love found in unexpected moments, blending slow-burn romance with heartfelt storytelling.

Her books have garnered over 58.2K reads on Wattpad, where she continues to connect with readers through character-driven stories and immersive worlds. When she’s not writing, she enjoys creating new story ideas and drawing inspiration from everyday moments.

Why I write

I write stories driven by fate, chance encounters, and emotional intensity. I’m drawn to characters who are shaped by loss, loyalty, and love found in the most unexpected moments. Whether set in the real world or in realms ruled by power and destiny, my stories explore connection, resilience, and the quiet strength it takes to choose love when everything else demands survival.

My Reach

My work has reached over 58.2K readers on Wattpad, where I continue to explore both contemporary and fantasy romance.
