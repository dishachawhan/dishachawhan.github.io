Where the Road Found Us

A solo vacation takes an unexpected turn when a woman riding through a silent road comes across a devastating accident. One decision—to stop, to help—pulls her into a night she never planned and a chain of events she can’t walk away from.

What begins with a stranger’s voice on a phone slowly leads her into an unfamiliar world of family, secrets, and a man who was never meant to matter—but does. A story of chance, connection, and a love born from the most unexpected detour.
