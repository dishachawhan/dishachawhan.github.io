Meet the Characters

Queen Gavreel

Human-born and unyielding in her convictions, she carries kindness as strength—not weakness. She stands for what is right even in a realm ruled by beasts and kings, proving that power does not always roar. Destined to rule not through fear, but through loyalty, she is the queen fate never meant to ignore.

King Lucifer

The most approachable of the triplet kings, Lucifer masks ruthless authority behind charm and wit. Playful with those he trusts and merciless to those who betray him, he is a ruler who proves that appearances are deceiving. His beast’s silver-spiked blue eyes are the last thing his enemies ever see.

King Demon

Silent, controlled, and terrifyingly efficient, Demon believes actions speak louder than words. His calm exterior hides a brutality reserved for traitors alone. When he moves, kingdoms fall—and his golden-eyed beast leaves no room for mercy.

King Azazel

Angel-faced and dangerously unhinged, Azazel is the most volatile of the triplets. Impulsive, aggressive, and cruel in his games, he delights in breaking his enemies long before ending them. His ruby-eyed beast burns with fury born from centuries of longing.
